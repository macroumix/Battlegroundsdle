How to Play


There are 4 clues to help you find the answer.

Start guessing but guess considerably, as you only have 6 guesses to find the answer.

First clue is the tier score of the minion.

Second clue is the type of the minion. E.g. Naga or Murloc.

Third clue is if the minion is available in Bob's Tavern or if it is a Token minion. (Yes the minion can be token :))

Final clue is the keywords of the minions text. This part is slightly tricky.

An example of which is: If the minion has deathrattle and summons a minion when it dies, the correct keywords for the minion are : (Deathrattle & Summon)

If the minion with deathrattle dies and doesn't summon anything the correct keywords for the minion is : Deathrattle

If the minion has a keyword "deathrattle" in its text but doesn't actually have a deathrattle like "Rabid Saurolisk" the correct keyword for the minion is still "Deathrattle".

Also, all reborn minions have summon in them as expected.

Keep all these in mind and have a great game :)
